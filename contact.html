Biography

Dr Alimatu-Saadia Yussiff currently lecture at the Department of Computer Science & Information Technology, University of Cape Coast, Ghana. Her research areas include, E-learning, E-collaboration, Information Systems (Business Informatics), Data Mining, Data modeling, Community of Inquiry Model (COI) and Internet of things. Her current project is on 'Transcript analysis of educational collaborative learning.'
